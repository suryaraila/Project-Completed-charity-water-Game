About the Prototype

This game uses a charity: water theme with blue and gold branding, interactive drop clicks, score tracking, a timer, and a challenge penalty for dry rocks.

What I built

A simple web game where players collect water drops and avoid hazard rocks to fill the well and score points.

How it works

Clickable elements update the score and trigger the win or penalty states. The timer and reset button keep gameplay moving.

Why it helps

The design emphasizes charity: water colors and theme while making the layout responsive on mobile and desktop devices.
